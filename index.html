What We Treat

Specialized Services

Azeem Hospital provides a full range of pediatric and specialist services under one roof in the heart of Multan.

🍼

Newborn & Neonatal Care

Comprehensive neonatal assessments, jaundice management, feeding support, and intensive newborn care by FCPS-certified neonatologists.

💉

Vaccinations & Immunization

Complete EPI and extended immunization schedules for children of all ages following Pakistan's national guidelines.

🫁

Pediatric Pulmonology

Expert diagnosis and management of asthma, bronchitis, pneumonia, and chronic respiratory conditions in children.

🩸

Pediatric Hematology

Evaluation and treatment of blood disorders including anemia, thalassemia, and related hematological conditions in children.

📈

Growth & Development

Monitoring physical, cognitive, and nutritional development milestones with early detection and intervention strategies.

🔬

Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery

Specialist surgical procedures for congenital abnormalities, burns, cleft palate, and reconstructive needs in pediatric patients.
